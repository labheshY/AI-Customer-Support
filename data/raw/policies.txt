Refund Policy:
Customers can request a refund within 7 days of delivery. The product must be unused and in original packaging.

Shipping Policy:
Orders are processed within 24 hours and shipped within 2–3 business days.

Cancellation Policy:
Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped. Once shipped, cancellation is not possible.

Return Policy:
Returns are accepted for eligible products within 7 days of delivery.

Replacement Policy:
Defective or damaged products can be replaced at no extra cost.
